Mission

Sheltering Silverton collaborates with neighbors and organizations to provide a framework that
supports, develops and provides services to, and advocates for our vulnerable neighbors in order for them to
transition toward fuller security, stability, and purpose within our community.

Day Center
The Day Center supports our unsheltered neighbors by providing community and basic needs , including case management, peer support, and connection to services.
Warming Shelter
The Warming Shelter will be open 7 days a week from November to February to individuals seeking shelter from harsh winter weather . The shelter also provides a hot dinner and breakfast to those sheltering there.
Transitional Housing
We support transitional housing pods to be located at local churches
