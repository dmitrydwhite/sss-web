Mission

Silverton Sheltering Services collaborates with neighbors and organizations to provide a framework that supports, develops and provides services to, and advocates for our vulnerable neighbors in order for them to transition toward fuller security, stability, and purpose within our community.

Vision

Silverton Sheltering Services (SSS) provides leadership and focus to a collaborative community response to the growing crisis of homelessness in the Silverton area. Through a diverse network of grassroots community partnerships, private and public initiatives, and collective hospitality, we aim to respond to the crisis of homelessness in Silverton.
